About

The world’s largest sports apparel and equipment makers run large closed and proprietary systems. They have absolute control over their value chains; from design, prices, and what markets they serve. While this control is standard practice for the co-op, it excludes users with incredible and extra creative power from participating in value creation and also excludes those in unserved markets, especially in emerging economies.
