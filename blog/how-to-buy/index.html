How to buy AVC tokens

March 2018 · 1 minute read
  1. Make sure you have an Ethereum wallet that is not from an cryptocurrency exchange for security reasons. If you want to buy ether for your wallet, we highly recommend an exchange like Binance. 
  2. Send ETH to the token address listed on the AVARC (https://avarc.co) homepage and make sure to check EtherScan to track your purchase and verify that it went through.
  3. Keep your AVC tokens safe until marketplace launch. You could download the Metamask plug-in for Google Chrome to secure your information and keys for future reference.
